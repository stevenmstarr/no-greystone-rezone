Frequently Asked Questions

What is Preserve the Greystone Community?

It is a resident-led initiative focused on protecting the neighborhood, trails, green space, safety, and long-term quality of life.

What are the major concerns?

Concerns include increased traffic, parking overflow, noise, environmental degradation, and strain on infrastructure.

How do I stay informed?

Check this website regularly for updates, hearing notices, and community meeting information.

Can I volunteer?

Yes. Volunteers are welcome to assist with outreach, event coordination, petition efforts, and communications.
